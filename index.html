Who We Are

Established in 2012, we opened our doors with a simple concept in mind: to provide Statesboro with a classy and fun smoke shop that offers a wide variety of smoking products that are backed up by the best priced we can offer, supported by outstanding customer service, and a friendly environment that is welcoming to everyone (over the age of 18, of course).



We carry a wide variety of products including hand pipes, glass water pipes, vaporizers, e-cigarettes and juices, incense, tapestries, roll-your-own papers, flavored cigar wraps, pipe tobacco, detox, smoking accessories, fun novelty items, and more. We have recently added in a cigar humidor and an expanding selection of premium cigars.



We offer monthly and weekly deals that can't be beat! Aside from our already competitive pricing that customer's love, we always have featured specials with free products just for shopping with us. Another crowd pleaser is our raffels, where one lucky winner walks away with a sophisticated water pipe with known brand names such as RooR and Medicali. Our hot deals and cool products are sure to keep you coming back for more!



While we aim to offer everything you need to enjoy your next smoking session, we get our ideas for new products and speciality items from suggestions made by our loyal, growing customer base and industry trade shows that we attend annually. We are constantly updating our invetory and product lines to keep custombers coming back for more. Stop by today and sign up for our Customer Loyalty Program and always receive the VIP treatment at A Smokin' Place.
